About the College

AMC Institutions, established under the Paramahamsa Foundation Trust, are committed to delivering quality education from the primary level to doctoral programs. Situated on a 52- acre eco-friendly campus along Bannerghatta Main Road, Bengaluru, the group includes AMC Engineering College (AMCEC), established in 1999. AMCEC is permanently affiliated with Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU), Belagavi, and holds several prestigious accreditations, including NAAC A+, NBA accreditation for three undergraduate programs under the Outcome-Based Education (OBE) format, and UGC 2(f) recognition. The institution was granted Autonomous status by VTU beginning with the academic year 2025â€“26. The college offers a comprehensive range of programs, including 11 undergraduate and 6 postgraduate engineering programs, as well as MBA and MCA. It also hosts seven VTU-approved research centers for M.Sc. (Engineering by Research) and Ph.D. studies. With a curriculum encompassing Computer Science, Information Science, Electronics & Communication, Mechanical, Civil, and Electrical Engineering, AMCEC blends academic rigor with practical learning. The institution emphasizes research, innovation, and skill development, supported by state-of-the-art laboratories and modern infrastructure. Beyond academics, AMCEC promotes holistic student development through active technical clubs, cultural activities, and sports. Strategic collaborations with industry and academic partners offer students hands-on experience through internships, live projects, and strong placement support, fostering a dynamic and industry-ready learning environment.
